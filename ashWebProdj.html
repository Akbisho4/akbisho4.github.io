Replication(Key)

For this assignment it was required that we simply pick an item and replicate it to size in Fusion 360. I had found a prop key that is about two and a half inches long. The process collectively took about three hours, but only because I was just learning the application. If I had to do it now, I could definitely do it in under an hour. Since we had to design our item to scale, I chose to design the key in a much larger size, and then rescale at the end. This way, I was not too stressed about sizing during the whole design process. Through this progect I gathered a firm understanding of basic tools and how to navigate the 3D space.


A BetterBirdhouse

Our assignment was to create a birdhouse for a specific species of bird. Our houses needed to be designed with protection against weather and predators in mind. I created my birdhouse for the Scarlet Tanger. The process of building the house in Fusion 360 took about four or five hours collectively. This assignment helped me to learnt the more complex elements of the application, for I have a better understanding of how to lay down materials as well as differenciating between new 3D surfaces that I created.

ArtRemix

Our assignment was to choose a renaissance era painting, and using tools in illustrator, paint over the original to create a remixed version of it. The goal was to highlight shifts in colors. In addition to color matching, we also had to frame a particular section of the painting to focus on, for we were developing our knowledge on composition. We wanted to take an already well composed painting and reframe it to create a new focal point.With this assignment, I learned a lot about the tools in illustrator as well as truly becoming familiar with key strokes. The assignment was very tedious, but very helpful in learning the ins and outs of illustrator.
